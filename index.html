About US

Sri.Manjunatha Education and Development Society(R)

Sri.Manjunatha Education and Development Society is non-profit, non-political and development organization. It has been working with multi sectoral communities without distinction of caste, creed and community in Bangalore Urban and Chamarajanagar Districts of Karnataka since its inception in 2004.
It has been working for development of the most marginalized and vulnerable sections of the society. The approach of the organization is to implement development programmes based on the felt needs of the people with an effort to mobilize people's paarticipation for individuals and community development.
